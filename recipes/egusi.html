Egusi Soup

How to make egusi soup

Egusi soup is a Nigerian soup made with egusi seeds, tomatoes, and spices. It is a popular soup in Nigeria and is often served with fufu.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup egusi seeds
  • 1/2 cup palm oil
  • 1/2 cup tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup onions
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup carrots
  • 1/2 cup celery
  • 1/2 cup garlic
  • 1/2 cup ginger
  • 1/2 cup scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. Soak the egusi seeds in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Blend the tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, carrots, celery, garlic, ginger, and scotch bonnet pepper.
  3. Heat the palm oil in a pot and add the blended ingredients. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the egusi seeds and salt. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the water and stir. Cover and cook for 20 minutes.
