Jollof Rice

How to Make Jollof Rice in 5 Easy Steps

Jollof Rice, a classic Nigerian rice dish flavored with tomatoes, peppers, and lots of seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup onions
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup carrots
  • 1/2 cup celery
  • 1/2 cup garlic
  • 1/2 cup ginger
  • 1/2 cup scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and soak in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil in a pot and add the onions, bell peppers, carrots, celery, garlic, ginger, and scotch bonnet pepper.
  3. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato paste, salt, and water. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the rice and stir. Cover and cook for 20 minutes.
